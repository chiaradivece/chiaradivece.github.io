Projects

  • 2020.09 - Present
    6D Pose Regression of Ultrasound Diagnostic Planes
    Developed a regression CNN to predict the 6D pose (3D rotations and translations) of a plane in relation to the anatomy using image features and a Unity-based simulation environment for automatic acquisition of supervised synthetic data. Achieved reliable localization of US planes within the fetal brain in phantom data with a median error of 0.90 mm/1.17° and successful generalization of pose regression for an unseen real fetal brain.
    • Deep Learning
    • Medical Image Analysis
    • Pytorch
    • Python
    • C#
  • 2020.02 - 2021.02
    Simulation of Brain Deformations in Keyhole Neurosurgery
    Developed a Unity-based realistic and time-bounded simulator that mocks brain deformations during keyhole surgical procedures. Validated using recorded deformation data of in-vivo animal trials, achieving a mean mismatch of 4.73±2.15%.
    • Unity
    • Simulation
    • Python
    • C#
  • 2019.01 - 2019.12
    Virtual Reality and Haptics for Surgical Skills Training
    Developed a VR and haptic-based simulator for urological training in obtaining the pneumoperitoneum. Improved the acquisition of psychomotor skills and achieved a 'good' usability score in the System Usability Scale test with experienced clinicians.
    • Segmentation
